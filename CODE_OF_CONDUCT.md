# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, trolling, or insulting comments
- Personal or political attacks
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by opening an issue or contacting maintainers directly. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated.
